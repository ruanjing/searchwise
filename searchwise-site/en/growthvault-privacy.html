Privacy Policy

GrowthVault processes and stores all materials locally

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Local-First Mode

GrowthVault is a local-first browser extension. All saved text clips, project categories, copywriting templates, and configuration options are stored directly in your local browser storage (Chrome Local Storage). **No data is uploaded or transmitted to any external servers.**

Data Collection & Usage

We **do not collect, transmit, or share** any of your personal information or browsing history:

  • **Personally Identifiable Information (PII)**: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or credentials.
  • **Location & Network Activity**: We do not collect your location, IP address, browsing history, or user activity logs.
  • **Website Content**: We only save web text clips and page URLs locally in your extension storage when you explicitly click "Save to GrowthVault".

Permission Usage Details

To provide its core functionality, the extension only requests the minimum necessary permissions:

  • `activeTab`: Used to capture the active tab's title and URL when you choose to save a clip, to automatically associate the clip with its source page.
  • `contextMenus`: Used to add the "Save to GrowthVault" action in the right-click menu, allowing you to quickly save selected text on any webpage.
  • `storage`: Used to securely store your clips and project structure locally on your device.

Commitment to Data Security

We guarantee that:

  • We will never sell or transfer your user data to third parties.
  • We will not use or transfer your data for any purpose unrelated to the extension's single purpose (saving and organizing growth materials).
  • We will not use your data for advertising, credit scoring, or lending purposes.
